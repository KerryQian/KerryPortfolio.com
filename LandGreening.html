Land Greening

In collaboration with Greening Australia

About

This project was a collaboration with Greening Australia, a non-profit organisation who's main goal is to restore and protect Australia's biodiversity. Our main goal for this project was to produce a prototype tool in grasshoper that would allow for users to see how the growth of vegetation (grass, bushes, & trees) would affect their land in terms of visual, and thermal comfort. Within the project, I was in charge of the modelling of vegetation growth as well as the generation of topography for the vegetation to grow from.

The modelling of the vegetation required that it would be possible to simulate the growth of vegetation on land within any set timeframe ranging from a sappling to a fully mature tree. In addition to this, I was also tasked with generating a topography of land given land lines and from there, simulate the seeding and growing of vegetation.
